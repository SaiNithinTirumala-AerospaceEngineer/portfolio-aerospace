About

I am an aeronautical engineer pursuing my MSc at the University of Bristol, with hands-on experience spanning aircraft maintenance analysis, turbomachinery manufacturing, VTOL aerodynamic design, and propulsion systems for unmanned aerial vehicles.

My major graduation project - Hybrid VTOL Wing Design using AI Algorithms - applied machine learning to aerodynamic optimisation of a vertical take-off and landing wing configuration, directly bridging aerospace engineering with AI-driven design exploration.

At CAMP Systems, I worked as an Associate Aircraft Analyst handling airworthiness directives, AME documentation, and maintenance compliance for commercial aviation clients. At BHEL Hyderabad, I studied steam and gas turbine manufacturing at industrial scale - giving me propulsion-level insight alongside my avionics work.

Targeting Airbus, BAE Systems, and Rolls-Royce for roles in avionics, aircraft structures, propulsion engineering, and systems integration from September 2026.
